Inspiration:
I am a huge fan of badminton, and represent my school and academy at a district level. Yet, my school bag does not represent the badminton, sporty side of me. So I wanted to create my own Badminton shuttle Keychain!

What is it:
This product is a 3D printed badminton keychain with a cool looking logo inside, 16 feathers, and a plastic cork. 

How to use:
Just 3D print the parts in the CAD files, 16 feathers and 1 cork. The feathers can be inserted into the cork just like lego. and thats doneeee!
